# Province Rule Checklist

Do not assume all provinces use the same志愿 model. Confirm the current official rule for the student's province and year.

## Must Confirm

- 批次设置 and whether本科/专科/提前批/特殊类型 are mixed or separate.
- 志愿单位: 院校, 院校专业组, 专业+院校, or another structure.
- 投档方式: parallel志愿, sequential志愿, 专业平行志愿, etc.
- 每批可填数量 and each unit's专业数量.
- 是否有专业调剂 and whether调剂 happens within school, group, or another unit.
- 选科限制 and再选科 requirements.
- 同分排序规则.
- 退档 consequences and征集志愿 process.

## New Gaokao Patterns

- `3+3`: usually requires checking each major's selected-subject requirement.
- `3+1+2`: separate物理类/历史类 lines plus再选科 constraints.
- 院校专业组模式: group-level risk is central; a safe school can still contain an unsafe group.
- 专业平行志愿模式: major-level matching matters more than broad school matching.

## Special Categories

Treat these as separate workflows and require official verification:

- 军校、公安、司法、航海、飞行、消防
- 公费师范、优师计划、定向医学生、地方专项、高校专项、国家专项
- 强基计划、综合评价、艺术、体育
- 中外合作、港澳院校、民办高收费、异地校区
