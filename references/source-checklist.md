# Source Checklist

Prefer official and current sources.

## High Trust

- 省级教育考试院/招生考试院
- 当年普通高校招生专业目录/计划书
- 院校本科招生网
- 院校招生章程
- 阳光高考平台

## Medium Trust

- Commercial志愿 platforms that clearly cite official data
- School counselor spreadsheets with source links
- News reports quoting official documents

## Low Trust

- Screenshots without source/date
- Social media summaries
- Unverified机构表格
- Old admission-score compilations without province/year/batch

## Verification Items

Before final submission, verify:

- province and year
- batch
- school code
- major group code
- major code
- plan count
- subject requirement
- tuition
- campus
- remarks and restrictions
- whether the record is school-level, group-level, or major-level
